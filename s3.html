Kingdom Mechanics

The Mithril-Sacred Water Economic Loop

This cycle is the engine of your seasonal progression. Master it or fall behind.

Mithril → Fountains → Sacred Water → Curse Lab → Curse Resistance → Defeat Enemies → Mithril

Curse Resistance (CR)

The most important stat for S3 PvE. If your CR is too low, your power is irrelevant. Increase it via the Curse Research Lab and the Weekly Pass (+250 CR). A rally leader's CR applies to all members—use this to your advantage!

The Desert Protector Trap

Protectors are powerful, single-use units. DO NOT WASTE THEM on early-game zombies. They are a finite resource intended for the most critical PvP battles: Spice Wars.

First 72-Hour Blueprint

Day One Action Plan

  1. Strategic Relocation: Move alliance hive near high-level objectives.
  2. Tactical Spending: Buy Battle/Weekly Pass for immediate resource and CR boosts.
  3. Coordinated Conquest: Rally with high-CR players to chain first-kill Mithril rewards.
  4. Activate Buffs: Use "War Fever" for a constant 1% attack boost.

Optimal Building Priority

  1. Blessing Fountains to Lvl 15: Your #1 Mithril priority to unlock more fountains.
  2. Curse Research Lab: Funnel all Sacred Water here to boost CR.
  3. Level Fountains Evenly: After all are unlocked, for best efficiency.
  4. Protector Altar: Prioritize Altar (strength) over the Protector's Field (capacity).

Upgrading Scarlett to UR (requires 1600 purple shards) makes her a top-tier tank for S3. However, her long-term value post-season is debated. Invest heavily if your goal is to win S3; consider alternatives if you're planning for S4 and beyond.

Alliance Warfare

Spice Wars: Tactical Primer

Spice Wars are the centerpiece of S3 PvP. Victory requires coordination, not just brute force. This is where you use your saved Desert Protectors.

PhaseObjectiveTactical Imperative
OffenseMaximize Spice PlunderDestroy ALL four outbuildings (5% Spice each) BEFORE attacking the main Alliance Center (10% Spice).
DefenseMinimize Spice LossStrongest players reinforce the main Center. Use shields (36h-72h) before the battle if you anticipate being a target.
StrategyLong-Term VictoryAvoid accumulating too much Spice early to stay in favorable competitive tiers. Do not "dogpile" an enemy with another alliance.

The Veteran's Edge

The Greenification Gambit

The most efficient way to gain Profession XP is not combat. Using 10 stamina to "greenify" a desert patch gives ~4,000 XP, roughly 6x more than fighting. This is the optimal path for leveling your profession, especially for F2P players.

Top 5 Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Wasting Protectors on early-game PvE.
  2. Neglecting Drones & core game power.
  3. Spreading Upgrades across too many heroes.
  4. Ignoring the Greenify XP Method for profession.
  5. Destroying the Spice War Center First & losing plunder.
