Weekly Event Guide & Tasks

Daily Must-Dos:
  • Collect Daily Chests, VIP Rewards, Freebies.
  • Like Arena Participants.
  • Hit World Boss (if up).
  • Kill Doom Walker (Lvl 100+ ideally). S2 Day 1: Kill *after* reset for Coal!
  • Spend all Stamina (Campaign, Radar).
  • 5 Radar Missions (esp. w/ Taylor).
  • Plunder Trucks/Secret Tasks (Shards, SSR Gear/Decor).
  • Honorable Campaign.
  • Alliance Helps, Resource Gathering (stay below VS cap).
  • Gather Coal & manage Furnace heat.
  • Claim hourly Coal from Dig Sites (Alliance Menu).
  • Use Buffs before major upgrades.

Remember to adapt based on current VS events!

Week 1: Cold Wave Alert (-20°C)

Focus: Setup, Resource Management, Initial Expansion.

Key Events: Purge Action, Cold Wave Incoming, Frost Boost, High-heat Furnace, Alliance Furnace, Titanium Alloy Factories, City Unlocks.

Daily Pointers:
  • Day 1 (Apr 13): Follow S2 quests. Build/Upgrade Furnace & 1st Factory. Gather Coal (zombies, events). Save Doom Walker kill for AFTER reset! Understand Temp/VR. Alliance Furnace setup. Identify Lv 1 Dig Sites (~2500+ VR needed).
  • Day 2 (Apr 14): Continue upgrades. Maximize Coal. Alliance attempts 1st Dig Site capture (Max 2/day). Attack Polar Beast (unfreeze?), reinforce (4hr timer). Claim hourly Coal (Alliance menu).
  • Days 3-7: Capture 2nd Dig Site. Continue Furnace/Factory upgrades. Unlock/upgrade more Factories. Prepare for Week 2. Research cost reduction tech.

Week 2: Cold Wave Alert (-30°C)

Focus: Increase VR & Heat, Defend Alliance Furnace, Capture more sites.

Key Events: Polar Dishes, Beast Crisis (Alliance Furnace defense).

Details: Start prioritizing VR tech and furnace levels. Coordinate alliance defense during Beast Crisis. Capture Lv 2/3 Dig Sites as VR allows.

Week 3: Cold Wave Alert (-50°C)

Focus: Upgrade Violet, Capture Lv 5/6 Cities, Push Furnace/VR.

Key Events: Hero Promotion (Violet SSR -> UR), Faction Awards, City Unlock (Lv 5/6).

Details: Use accumulated shards/passes for Violet promotion. Rally for higher-level City captures. Continue pushing Furnace/Factory levels aggressively.

Week 4: Cold Wave Alert (-70°C)

Focus: Participate in Rare Soil War, Understand Nuclear Furnace.

Key Events: Rare Soil War begins, Nuclear Furnace introduced.

Details: Learn the Rare Soil War mechanics (declaration, invites, war phase). Coordinate attacks/defenses. Start planning for Nuclear Furnace resource needs.

See War Cycle

Week 5: Cold Wave Alert (-90°C)

Focus: Manage extreme cold, Continue Rare Soil War.

Key Events: Turn on Nuclear Furnace.

Details: Ensure sufficient heat generation. Continue active participation in Rare Soil War. Allocate resources to activate and upgrade the Nuclear Furnace.

Week 6: Cold Wave Alert (-110°C)

Focus: Major push for Rare Soil ranking, PvP/Capture activity.

Key Events: Rare Soil Showdown.

Details: This is a critical week for rankings. Maximize participation in Rare Soil events and related PvP. Ensure highest possible VR/Heat.

Week 7: Cold Wave Alert (-130°C)

Focus: High participation in Faction Duel.

Key Events: Faction Duel.

Details: Coordinate with your faction for the final major PvP event. Ensure troops are healed and ready.

Week 8: End of Season

Focus: Collect rewards, Prepare for next phase.

Key Events: Taste of Victory, Final Rankings.

Details: Claim all seasonal rewards. Review performance and plan for the off-season or next event.
