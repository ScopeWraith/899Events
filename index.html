There is an efficient trick to squeeze more VS points out of Troop creation and upgrades. Ideally, you can structure your Barracks so that each one upgrades troops to the next tier. For instance, after a level 23 Barracks produces a batch of T7 Troops, the level 26 Barracks would upgrade those troops to T8. A level 29 Barracks would then upgrade that same batch from T8 to T9. Lastly, the level 30 Barracks would bring them to T10.

Doing this allows you to produce troops for each tier, and essentially double, triple, or quadruple the points, depending on how many upgrade levels you have structured with your Barracks.
