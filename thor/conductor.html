How it works!

This tool is designed to select a "Train Conductor" from a pool of alliance members based on a weighted random selection. The goal is to create a fair, transparent, and balanced rotation that rewards active and high-ranking members while still giving everyone a chance.

  • Fairness First: The system uses a "Luck" score to weigh the odds. Higher luck means a better chance of being selected, but it never guarantees it.
  • Cooldown System: After a member serves as conductor, they enter a cooldown period to ensure others get a turn.
  • Rewarding Performance: Higher ranks and special medals provide bonuses to a member's luck and can reduce their cooldown time, rewarding dedication and achievement.
  • Admin Management: Logged-in administrators have full control over the member list, selection history, and medal awards to keep the system up-to-date.

The Drawing

When an admin clicks the "Choose Conductor" button, the system performs a weighted random drawing from all eligible members.

  • Eligibility: A member is considered eligible if they are not on cooldown. If all members are on cooldown, this rule is ignored and everyone becomes eligible.
  • Luck Score: Each eligible member's chance is determined by their total Luck score. The formula is essentially 1 + Rank Luck + Medal Luck + Sub Boost. This score becomes their "weight" in the drawing.
  • The Pick: The system chooses a winner from the eligible pool based on these weights. A member with a 100 Luck score has roughly double the chance of being picked as a member with a 50 Luck score.
  • Pending Confirmation: The chosen member is not final until an admin clicks "Lock In". This allows for re-rolls if the selected member is unavailable.
  • Re-rolling: If the "Re-roll" button is used, the pending conductor is returned to the pool for the next drawing without any penalty or bonus, and a new conductor is chosen from the remaining eligible members.
  • Substitution Boost: If a chosen conductor is substituted (a feature no longer present on the main UI but kept in the logic), they are put back into the pool and receive a temporary +50% "Sub Boost" to their luck. This boost is removed the next time they are successfully locked in as conductor.

Admin Responsibilities

To access administrative controls, you must log in using the provided credentials. Admins are responsible for maintaining the accuracy of the tool.

  • Manage Members: Add new members, edit their name and rank, or remove them from the alliance list via the "Members" tab.
  • Award Medals: Award medals to members to grant them temporary bonuses. This can be done from the "Medals" tab or from the member edit screen.
  • Designate Conductor: Manually set a member as the conductor by clicking their name in the "Members" list to open the Player Information Card and using the "Designate" button.
  • Manage History: Manually add or delete past conductor records from the "History" tab to correct errors or fill in missing data.
