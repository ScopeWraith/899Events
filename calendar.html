Capture Mechanics
  • Dig Sites/Cities: Require defeating Polar Beast (Needs VR, Rally).
  • Process: Reinforce after beast defeat (Max 10 squads). 4-hour timer. First to 100% wins.
  • Limits: Max 4 Dig Sites owned. Max 2 Dig Site captures per day.
  • Protection: Captured sites have 36hr protection.
