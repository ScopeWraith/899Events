Season 2 Kick-Off TONIGHT: Let's Conquer the Polar Storm!

Hey Team,

Get ready! Season 2: Polar Storm officially begins tonight! We're diving into a frozen map where managing temperature and new resources is key.

Our Starting Point:

We will begin together on the edge of the map, starting from the node located furthest west of the Capitol.

Day 1 Priorities - What to Focus On:

  • Build Your Furnaces:
    • Unlock your personal High-heat Furnace immediately. This is crucial for base temperature and Virus Resistance. Start upgrading it using Titanium Alloy.
    • Build your first Titanium Alloy Factory. Collect Coal to build and upgrade it. Strategy: Focus on getting one factory to level 15 before building the next one.
    • Keep your base warm! Use your furnace (especially during Blizzards - check the auto-on settings) and stay near the Alliance Furnace when possible.
  • Repair the Alliance Furnace:
    • Find the Alliance Furnace marker on the map (Alliance -> Alliance Furnace -> Coordinates).
    • Send one squad to help repair it as soon as you can.
    • Donate Coal to the Alliance Furnace whenever possible to keep it running for everyone.
  • Gather COAL:
    • Coal is VITAL for furnaces and building upgrades.
    • Get it from: Killing zombies (first-time level kills give more!), daily Doom Walker first blood kills, Season quests, and events like the Cold Wave Alert.
  • Virus Resistance:
    • Upgrade your High-heat Furnace to increase your Virus Resistance. This is essential for fighting the Mutant Beasts guarding Dig Sites.
  • Teamwork is Crucial:
    • Heat Boost: Send Recon Planes to allies' bases to help them warm up (+2°C per hit). Getting everyone to 40°C briefly unlocks Battle Pass rewards.
    • De-Ice: Help clear ice from any ally bases that get frozen.

Our First Major Target:

Once we're set up, our first objective will be to capture the Level 1 Dig Site located furthest west of the Capitol. These sites provide Coal over time. Prepare your troops and boost your Virus Resistance! We will coordinate the capture efforts in Alliance chat.

Let's communicate, help each other stay warm, gather those resources, and make a strong start to Season 2! Check chat frequently for updates and coordination.

Let's dominate!
